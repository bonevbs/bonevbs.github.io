Welcome! I am a Senior Research Scientist at NVIDIA Research in Zurich, working on AI for science — bridging rigorous numerical methods and scalable machine learning. I pioneered [Spherical Fourier Neural Operators](https://developer.nvidia.com/blog/modeling-earths-atmosphere-with-spherical-fourier-neural-operators/) and contribute to the [FourCastNet](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.12144) family of weather models, with research adopted at operational scale (NOAA, ECMWF, and others).

I earned my [Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics]({{ '/files/thesis_compressed.pdf' | relative_url }}) from [EPFL](https://www.epfl.ch) under Prof. Jan S. Hesthaven. I also hold degrees in Physics and Aerospace Engineering from the University of Stuttgart. My [research]({{ '/research/' | relative_url }}) spans neural operators, sparse solvers for wave problems, and discontinuous Galerkin methods for geophysical flows. See [software]({{ '/software/' | relative_url }}) for more.

I have been fortunate to collaborate with Anima Anandkumar, Thorsten Kurth, Jan S. Hesthaven, Jaideep Pathak, Karthik Kashinath, and many others.
